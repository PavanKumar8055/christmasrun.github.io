How To Play?
1. Just tap the Screen to let the Santa jump.
2. Collect as many gifts as you can, without fall.
3. Run as long as you can and collect all the gifts.

Features of the game
1.Simple and fun game
2.Beautiful graphics/ Music
3.Smooth Touch controls
4.Tap the screen to make Santa Run.
5.Collect all the Christmas gifts for the kids.
6.Have the music and sound effects
